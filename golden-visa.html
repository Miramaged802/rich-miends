What is the Golden Visa?

Understanding the UAE's long-term residency program

Long-term Residency Program

The Golden Visa is a 5 or 10-year renewable residency visa granted to selected categories without the need for a sponsor, with automatic renewal possibilities. It's one of the UAE's most prominent initiatives to encourage investment and attract global talent.
