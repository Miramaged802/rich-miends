What is the Remote Working Visa in Dubai?

It is a one-year residency visa that allows individuals living outside the UAE to live in Dubai while working remotely for companies or clients based outside the country.

Remote Work Visa vs Freelancer Visa

Understanding the key differences between both visa types

Remote Work Visa

  • For working with companies outside UAE
  • No local freelance license required
  • One year validity, renewable

Freelancer Visa

  • For independent work within UAE
  • Requires freelance license from Free Zone
  • 1-2 years validity depending on authority
