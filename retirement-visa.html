What is the Retirement Visa in Dubai?

It is a renewable 5-year residency visa for retired residents or foreigners aged 55 and above, allowing them to live in Dubai if they meet financial eligibility.

5-Year Validity

Long-term residency with renewal option

Who Can Apply?

Retirement visa eligibility criteria for retirees seeking Dubai residency

Age Requirement

Retirees aged 55 years or older, whether from inside or outside the UAE.

Work Background

Former employees from public or private sector with documented retirement status.

Residency Status

Open to both UAE residents and international retirees looking to relocate to Dubai.

Financial Planning

Financial Requirements

Choose one of the following financial options to qualify

Property Ownership

Own property in Dubai worth at least AED 1 million

Financial Savings

Savings of at least AED 1 million in a UAE bank account

Monthly Income

Monthly income of at least AED 20,000

Combined Option

AED 500,000 savings + property worth AED 500,000

Required Documents

Essential documentation needed for your retirement visa application

Valid Passport

Valid passport with minimum 6 months validity remaining.

Retirement Proof

Official retirement certificate or documentation from employer.

Financial Proof

Bank statements, property deeds, or income documentation.

Health Insurance

Valid health insurance policy covering the UAE.

Passport Photo

Recent passport-sized photograph with white background.

Dubai Residence

Property ownership or rental agreement in Dubai.
